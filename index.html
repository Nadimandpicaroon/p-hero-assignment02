Why you should do physical exercise?

There are so many reason to do physical exercise. Physical exercise is crucial for maintaining overall health and well-being. It also keep ourself fresh and active

Improves Physical Health

It enhances muscle strength, flexibility, and endurance, reducing the risk of injuries and improving mobility. Weight-bearing exercises like walking or resistance training strengthen bones, reducing the risk of osteoporosis and fractures.

Enhances Mental Health

Exercise has been shown to enhance brain function, improve memory, and protect against cognitive decline as we age. Exercise triggers the release of endorphins, which are natural mood lifters. It also reduces levels of stress hormones like cortisol.

Reduces Risk of Heart Disease

Regular exercise reduces the risk of atherosclerosis, a condition characterized by the buildup of plaque in the arteries that can lead to heart attacks and strokes. By improving cholesterol levels, reducing blood pressure, and promoting healthy blood flow, exercise lowers the risk of developing coronary artery disease.
